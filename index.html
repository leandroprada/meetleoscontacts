About the project

Many ideas go unrealized, I have them right before falling asleep, and by the morning, they are gone. This one is not lost. I decided I'd help out my contacts providing a space for them to tell the rest of my connections what they do, and how they can help.

I'm providing this space as I think video makes a difference, and it is the closest we have to real life meeting, at least until we go into the metaverse, right?

Recording your video

It has not many requirements, just that it be Landscape if possible and no longer than 3 minutes.

Nothing fancy, just try to keep it steady, choose some cool background, and stand out!

Topics

As for the topics, since this is for you, it's open to your likings. People choose what to say, some speak about upcoming webinar, some about their normal offering, or how they help others. Some just talk about what they love to do at work, or in life in general.
